Our Story

Making the web
more accessible.

Sorti started with a frustration: why are URL shorteners so complicated? Ads, paywalls, and expiring links seemed to be the norm.

We built Sorti to be the antidote. Open-source, privacy-focused, and designed with a "less is more" philosophy. We believe essential web tools should be invisible helpers, not attention seekers.
