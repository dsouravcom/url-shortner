About Sorti

Our mission is to make sharing links simple, secure, and accessible to everyone.

Our Story

Sorti was founded in 2025 with a simple goal: to provide a free, ad-free URL shortening service that puts users first. Frustrated with existing services that were cluttered with ads and tracking, our team set out to build a better solution.

Today, Sorti serves millions of users worldwide, helping individuals and businesses share links more effectively without compromising on privacy or user experience.

Why Choose Sorti?

  • No Ads: We believe in an ad-free internet experience.

  • Privacy Focused: We don't sell your data or track your activity.

  • Open Source: Our code is transparent and available for review.

  • Community Supported: Powered by users like you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Sorti.

Is Sorti really free?

Yes, Sorti is completely free to use with no hidden costs. Users can contribute to keeping the service running.

How long do shortened URLs last?

Your shortened URLs will never expire as long as Sorti exists. We're committed to maintaining all links indefinitely.

How does Sorti make money?

We don't. Sorti is supported entirely by voluntary contributions from our community. We believe in keeping the internet free and open without resorting to ads or selling user data.
